Resurrecting Waine

This is a piece of creative writing presented to you utterly raw.

August 29th, 2013

kill me waine

the boy walks down the stone staircase

there are no lights it is dark

still he could see sort of his outline

legs bent arms tied facing up against the wall

waine

he spoke to him

it is your turn

he untied his arms

i am dead already

waine looked him in the eyes

once brown eyes now brimmed with black

are met with black pearls

no

you are not dead

then kill me waine

kill me so you can have

everything i ever wanted

there was no hesitation

as they both reached for the blade

and together they drove it into his stomach

he slipped onto the floor

waine made his way up the stairs

the lights on the surface dived

into his black pearl eyes deep

to the bottom

and is gone

i am the monster

i am seeing myself now

she is clenching her chest with no breath

the blackness in her pupils are spreading

and dripping down her cheeks

the memories flash back

my fears suddenly creep up my throat

and become real monsters help

i changed myself to stop this

i killed him so i could take his place

and stop this from ever happening to me

but once again i see him crying now

she is crying now

it is happening to me again

except this time

i am the monster
