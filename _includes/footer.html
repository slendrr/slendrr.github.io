about me

I first learned about Bitcoin in 2012 and got involved to see how it all works. I learned to create wallets, fiddled around with exchanges, and even made an online purchase in Bitcoin! After the Mt. Gox crash, I took a break away from cryptocurrencies. Since then, many new and exciting developments have entered the cryptocurrency space. This is a place for me to keep track of what I've learned.
